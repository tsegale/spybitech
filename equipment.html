IT EQUIPMENT CLEANING

Where Spybitech started: professional cleaning of computers, labs and server rooms.

Dust is the leading cause of overheating in computer hardware. We clean workstations, server cabinets and entire computer labs using correct equipment and processes, not just compressed air over a desk.

  • Workstation Deep Cleaning

  • Computer Laboratory Cleaning

  • Server Room Cleaning

    Cabinet and floor cleaning under live conditions without powering down.

  • Scheduled Maintenance Contracts

    Quarterly or half-yearly contracts to keep equipment cooling-efficient.
