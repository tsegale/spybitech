NETWORKING

The infrastructure layer everything else depends on.

We plan and install switches, cabinets, routing and structured cabling so the network performs the way the rest of your IT investment needs it to.

NETWORK SWITCHES

The backbone of every local network, specified to the port count and speed you actually need.

We supply and configure managed and unmanaged switches from TP-Link and other trusted brands, sized to current users with headroom for growth and VLAN segmentation where required.

Get a quote

  • Managed & Unmanaged Options

    Simple plug-in switches through to full managed VLAN-capable units.

  • PoE Switch Options

    Power cameras, phones and access points directly over the network cable.

  • VLAN Segmentation

    Traffic separated by department or device type on managed installs.

  • Rack-Mount & Desktop Formats

    Cabinet-mounted or free-standing depending on the installation.

SERVERS & SERVER CABINETS

Compute and storage housed properly, not stacked on a shelf.

We supply, configure and rack-mount servers and UPS units in lockable cabinets with correct cable management. The result is a server room that a technician can work in without it being a problem to diagnose.

Plan your server room

  • Server Supply & Configuration

    Tower and rack-mount servers specified to your workload and budget.

  • Cabinet Installation

    Lockable open-frame and closed rack cabinets in standard U sizes.

  • UPS & Power Backup

    Uninterruptible power supply matched to the rack load for clean shutdowns.

  • Cable Management

    All patch cables labelled, colour-coded and routed through cable management arms.

FIREWALLS

Network perimeter security that actually reflects your traffic patterns.

A generic out-of-box firewall config leaves most ports open. We configure rule sets based on the services your business actually runs, tighten inbound and outbound policies, and document every change.

Review your firewall

  • Hardware Firewall Supply

    Purpose-built appliances rather than relying on a router's basic NAT.

  • Rule-Set Configuration

    Inbound and outbound rules written to your actual service profile.

  • VPN Gateway Setup

    Site-to-site and remote-access VPN tunnels configured and tested.

  • Ongoing Firmware Updates

    We track CVEs and push firmware patches before vulnerabilities are exploited.

WIRED / WIRELESS ROUTERS & MODEMS

Routing done once, correctly, so it does not become a recurring support call.

We supply and configure TP-Link and other brand routers with static addressing, correct DNS, QoS rules and separate SSID for guest access where required. No default passwords left in place.

Get a quote

  • Wired & Wireless Options

    Gigabit routers and Wi-Fi 6 APs selected to the coverage area.

  • Static IP & DNS Configuration

    Proper addressing schemes set from the start to avoid future conflicts.

  • QoS Prioritisation

    Voice and critical traffic prioritised over bulk downloads in the config.

  • TP-Link Equipment Supply

    Authorised TP-Link hardware with local warranty cover on all units.

STRUCTURED CABLING

Every run documented, every port labelled, every connection tested.

We install Cat5e and Cat6 structured cabling in trunking with patch panels and face plates, then test and certify each run. You get a cabling plant the next technician can actually read.

Plan your cabling

  • Cat5e Installation

    Cost-effective for 100Mbps networks with runs up to 100m.

  • Cat6 Installation

    1Gbps rated for all new installs where the budget allows it.

  • Patch Panel & Face Plates

    Terminates cleanly at both ends for a tidy, maintainable installation.

  • Testing & Documentation

    Each run tested with a cable certifier and labelled on both ends.

CAT6A & FIBRE OPTIC CABLES

High-speed backbone for buildings where copper is not enough.

For long inter-building runs, high-density data centres or 10Gbps backbone requirements, we install Cat6A and single/multi-mode fibre with fusion spliced terminations and OTDR testing.

Get a fibre quote

  • Cat6A to 10Gbps

    Augmented Cat6A rated to 500MHz for 10 Gigabit Ethernet links.

  • Single-Mode Fibre

    Long inter-building or campus backbone runs up to kilometres.

  • Multi-Mode Fibre

    Short-range data centre links with cost-effective transceivers.

  • Fusion Splicing & OTDR Test

    Every fibre splice tested with an OTDR trace supplied with the report.
